Common Questions

Before you reach out.

How quickly will I hear back?

Most quote requests get a personal reply within 24 hours on business days. If your project is urgent, calling is faster than email.

Is the quote really free?

Yes — initial consultations and written quotes are free with no obligation. We only charge for design work or detailed plans, and only after you approve those services in writing.

What information should I include in my message?

Project type (renovation, leasehold improvement, etc.), location, approximate timeline, rough budget range if you have one, and any photos of the existing space. The more we know, the more useful our first response will be.

Do you handle permits?

Yes. For projects that require permits, we manage the application process, drawings, and inspections as part of the project scope. You won't need to deal with city offices.

How far out are you booked?

It depends on the project size and time of year. Smaller renovations can sometimes start within 2–4 weeks; larger projects typically book 6–12 weeks out. We'll give you an honest timeline in our first conversation.

Prefer to talk?

Pick up the phone — we answer during business hours, and return after-hours calls the next morning.
