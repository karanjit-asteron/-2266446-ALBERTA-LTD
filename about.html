Our Story

The right team makes all the difference.

2266446 Alberta Ltd was founded on a simple belief: construction shouldn't be stressful.

Too many homeowners and business tenants have lived through projects that ran late, blew past budget, or left them chasing tradespeople for weeks. We knew there was a better way to do this.

From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, you'll have one team coordinating every trade, every permit, and every detail. We communicate clearly, we keep our timelines, and we finish what we start.

That commitment has earned us repeat clients, referrals, and a reputation across Alberta for showing up, doing the work, and standing behind it.
